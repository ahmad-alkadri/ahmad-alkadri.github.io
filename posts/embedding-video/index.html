Embedding video on Rmarkdown using Blogdown package

Posted on 2019, Nov 19 One min read

I’ve just read this article about embedding YouTube videos using this simple blogdown function:

blogdown::shortcode("youtube", "P3aGk_Gwqjw")

where "P3aGk_Gwqjw" is the video’s YouTube ID.

And that’s all. No wasted lines; very efficient. I think I will never cease to be amazed by R developers. The possibilities are truly endless now.
