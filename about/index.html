About

Hi! You can call me Ahmad, or Alkadri. No worries. We don't have first-name family-name in Indonesia. My friends in France call me Ahmad, but my friends in my hometown used to call me Adri. Or Al. Anyway, I use R very regularly for my work. Not only that, I also develop some R packages. You can check them out on my Github page. I started using R three years ago during an internship where I had to conduct many statistical analysis on a large volume of data and when I found Excel had become too taxing for the task, my colleague introduced me to R. It was quite difficult to learn at first, but now I kinda like it. No, scratch that, I love it.

Presently, I'm studying for my Ph.D. degree and working as an R&D Researcher at the same time at Henri Selmer Paris, a world leading manufacturer for wind musical instrument. For more complete picture of what I do professionaly, you can check out my LinkedIn.

In my spare time, I play guitar and harmonica. During weekends, I often go for roadtrips. I love blues, jazz, and a bit of electro-pop. Tip: google "Electro Swing". Check it out. Thank me later.

If you'd like to contact me, hit me on my email or my LinkedIn. You can even follow me on Instagram. So see you!
