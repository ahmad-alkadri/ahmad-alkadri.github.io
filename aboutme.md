Hi! You can call me Ahmad, or Alkadri. 
No worries. We don’t have first-name or 
family-name in Indonesia. My friends in France call me Ahmad, 
but my friends in my hometown used to call me Adri. 
Or Al. 

Anyway, I use [R](https://www.r-project.org) 
very regularly for my work. 
Not only that, but I also develop some R packages. 
You can check them out on my 
[Github page](http://github.com/ahmad-alkadri/). 
I started using R three years ago during an internship 
where I had to conduct much statistical analysis 
on a large volume of data and when I found Excel 
had become too taxing for the task, 
my colleague introduced me to R. 
It was quite difficult to learn at first, 
but now I kinda like it. No, scratch that, I love it.

Presently, I’m studying for my Ph.D. degree at 
the [Laboratory of Mechanics and Civil Engineering](http://www.lmgc.univ-montp2.fr/), 
Montpellier, and working 
as an R&D Researcher at the same time at 
[Henri Selmer Paris](http://www.selmer.fr/), 
a world-leading manufacturer for wind musical instrument. 
For a more complete picture of what I do professionally, 
you can check out 
[my LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alkadri/).

In my spare time, I play guitar and harmonica. 
During weekends, I often go on road trips. 
I love blues, jazz, and a bit of electro-pop.

Thank me later.
